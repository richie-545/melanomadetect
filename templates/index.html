About MelanoCheck

Melanoma is a serious form of skin cancer that originates in the pigment-producing cells called melanocytes. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment.

Benign lesions are non-cancerous growths that do not spread, whereas malignant lesions are cancerous and may invade surrounding tissues. MelanoCheck uses advanced AI to analyze images of skin lesions and provide a preliminary risk assessment.

This tool is intended for testing and educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.

How MelanoCheck Works

MelanoCheck uses a deep learning model that has been trained on thousands of skin lesion images. The model first extracts features using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) and then refines these features with Transformer-based layers. This enables the model to detect subtle differences between benign and malignant lesions.
